# AAS-EPD Mini-Grant — 2026

The AAS Education and Public Engagement Division
([AAS-EPD](https://aas.org/education/aas-epd-mini-grant-program))
runs an annual Mini-Grant program with small awards (typically up to
\$1,000) for member-led astronomy education and public-engagement
projects.

Low effort, low award size — but a useful CV line, a way to engage
the AAS-EPD community (which is critical for [DBER co-author
cultivation](../papers/index.md#co-author-leads)), and an
opportunity to position Sophie in the astronomy-education world.

## Program quick facts

- **Funder**: American Astronomical Society — Education and Public
  Engagement Division
- **Award**: Typically up to \$1,000
- **Eligibility**: AAS-EPD members (verify Anna's membership status)
- **Submission**: Annual call (typically summer); watch the AAS-EPD
  newsletter and aas.org/education
- **Effort**: ~1 day to write
- **Decision**: ~2–3 months

## Why apply

- **Low effort, high CV-line ratio.** A funded mini-grant is a real
  CV entry for ~1 day of writing.
- **AAS-EPD community engagement.** The mini-grant process puts you
  in the AAS-EPD network — exactly where [potential DBER
  co-authors](../papers/index.md#co-author-leads) live.
- **Positions Sophie in the astronomy-education space.** A funded
  AAS-EPD project tagged "Sophie" surfaces the platform in the
  community.
- **Bridge funding.** Even \$1K can pay for conference travel or a
  small infrastructure expense.

## Proposal idea

A reasonable mini-grant proposal: **a Sophie-built ASTR 101
sky-measurements demo released as an AAS-EPD community resource.**

Concrete deliverables:

- One Sophie-authored, interactive sky-measurement chapter
  (parallax, angular size, magnitudes — whatever fits a single
  module).
- Hosted on Sophie infrastructure, openly accessible.
- Documented for adoption by other instructors.
- Demoed at an AAS-EPD venue (workshop, webinar, summer meeting
  poster session).

The proposal scope is intentionally small — a single chapter, single
deliverable, simple metrics. AAS-EPD reviewers respond well to
focused, achievable mini-grant projects.

## Tactical notes

- **Verify AAS membership status** and AAS-EPD division enrollment.
  If not yet a member, joining is inexpensive and unlocks this and
  several other AAS-EPD opportunities.
- **Watch the AAS-EPD newsletter** for the 2026 call posting. Calls
  typically post in early-to-mid summer.
- **Don't wait for funding to engage AAS-EPD.** The community is the
  real prize; the \$1K is a side benefit. Volunteer for workshops,
  attend AAS-EPD division meetings at AAS summer meeting.

## Timeline

- **Summer 2026**: watch for call, apply when call opens (likely
  ~July–August).
- **Fall 2026**: decision; if funded, begin work in winter 2026 or
  spring 2027.
- **Spring 2027**: deliverable.
