# Strategy

How Sophie becomes a tenure-strengthening research program, a funded
open-source platform, and (eventually, post-tenure) an optional
commercial product — without any of those three things eating the
others.

This section is internal-facing. It captures the *why* and *when* of
Sophie as a professional artifact alongside its technical
architecture. The technical roadmap lives in
[Status → Roadmap](../status/roadmap.md); this section is the
research-program roadmap that funds and justifies the technical one.

## Operating constraints

Two constraints shape every decision in this section:

1. **Pre-tenure status.** Anna is a year-3 Assistant Professor of
   Astronomy at SDSU. Reappointed May 2026; tenure decision in roughly
   2–3 years. Every Sophie hour should ideally do *double-duty*:
   generate a publication, generate funding, or generate measurable
   teaching-effectiveness evidence. Hours that don't do double-duty are
   expensive.

2. **HITL mandate.** Per the project's
   [HITL mandate](../../../CLAUDE.md), strategic decisions are
   collaborative; this section captures decisions Anna has approved,
   not autonomous proposals. Open questions are surfaced explicitly
   throughout.
