Why this course is different

This is not a tour of the cosmos. ASTR 201 teaches you to think like an astronomer.

◐

Observation → Inference

Estimate a black hole's mass from orbital data; derive a star's temperature from its color. The sky gives you light — you extract the physics.

∑

A toolkit that travels

Dimensional analysis, the ratio method, order-of-magnitude estimation — reasoning skills that outlast any single equation.

◇

Models you pressure-test

Build a simplified model, then ask what it assumes and where it breaks — and what you can still trust when it does.
