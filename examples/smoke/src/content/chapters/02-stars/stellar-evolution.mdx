A star's life is determined almost entirely by its initial mass. The
H–R diagram organizes stars by luminosity and surface temperature; a
star's trajectory through that diagram traces its evolution from
protostar to main sequence to whichever ending (white dwarf, neutron
star, black hole) its mass earns.

The real chapter would walk that trajectory in detail.
